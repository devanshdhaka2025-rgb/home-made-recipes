Alfredo Pasta

Preparation Time: 50 mins

Difficulty: hard

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Thick cream
  • Nuts
  • fruits
  • Sweetner
  • flavour essence
  • Corn starch
  • eggs

Instructions

  1. Heat Dairy: In a saucepan, heat 1 1/4 cups milk and heavy cream until small bubbles form around the edge.
  2. Combine Ingredients: Whisk together the remaining 1 cup milk, sugar, and cornstarch.
  3. Thicken: Slowly whisk the sugar mixture into the hot cream. Simmer for 6–8 minutes, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  4. Temper Egg: Slowly pour some hot mixture into the egg yolk while whisking constantly, then pour it all back into the saucepan.
  5. Cool & Chill: Remove from heat, stir in vanilla. Cool completely and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  6. Churn: Churn in an ice cream maker for 15–30 minutes.
