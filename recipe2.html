Pizza

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: moderate

Ingredients

  • dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Meat
  • Bell Pepper
  • Other vegetable as per requirement

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix warm water, honey or sugar, and active dry yeast; let it sit until bubbly (5-10 mins).
  2. Combine this with flour, salt, and olive oil, then knead for 6–8 minutes until soft and elastic.
  3. Rise and Rest: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise until doubled in size, about 1–2 hours.
  4. Prepare Oven & Toppings: Preheat your oven to its highest setting—ideally
  5. with a pizza stone or heavy baking sheet inside for at least 45 minutes. Prepare your sauce and toppings.
  6. Shape the Dough: Divide the dough, then stretch it into a 10-12 inch circle on a floured surface, leaving the edges thicker.
  7. Assemble: Place the stretched dough onto a pizza peel dusted with flour or semolina. Spread a thin layer of sauce,
  8. followed by cheese and toppings. Avoid overloading, which makes the crust soggy.
  9. Bake: Slide the pizza onto the hot stone/pan and bake for 8–12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
