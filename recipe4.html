Pizza

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

  • Fresh
  • Crisp greens
  • Tangy vinaigrettes
  • Pepperoncini
  • Provolone
  • Olives

Instructions

  1. Greens: Use a mix of crunchy romaine and bitter radicchio for texture and color.
  2. Tangy Add-ins: Incorporate pepperoncini, kalamata olives, or banana peppers for a bold, tangy flavor.
  3. Proteins & Cheese: Add cubed salami, provolone, chickpeas, or mozzarella balls for a filling meal.
  4. Dressing: Whisk 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil with 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and optionally, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard for emulsification.
  5. Assembly: Chop all ingredients, combine in a large bowl, and toss with dressing immediately before serving to keep greens crisp.
