Alfredo Pasta

Preparation Time: 40-50 mins

Difficulty: Moderate-Easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • parmesan cheese
  • romano cheese
  • pepper
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta buy letting it sit in the container with hot water for a while
  2. Cook sauce by addind tomato sauce and other ingridents to a pan and stir over little heat to reach a sauce like consistency
  3. Mix together the boiled pasta along withe the sauce that was prepared
  4. Serve hot after grating the cheese on top of the pasta and enjoy!!!!!!
