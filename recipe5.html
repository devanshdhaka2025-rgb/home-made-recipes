Pizza

Preparation Time: 1 hr

Difficulty: hard

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • Crisp greens
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Ricotta
  • Egg yolks
  • All purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Roll: Divide dough into 4 portions. Roll each out to 1/32-inch thickness (very thin) using a pasta machine or rolling pin, creating long, thin sheets.
  2. Fill: Place 1-2 teaspoons of filling at 1.5-inch intervals along one sheet of pasta.
  3. Seal: Brush water or egg wash around the filling mounds. Place a second sheet of pasta over the top, pressing firmly around the filling to remove all air and seal the edges.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a gentle boil. Cook ravioli in batches for 2-4 minutes. They are done shortly after they float to the top.
  5. Serve with brown butter and sage, or a simple marinara sauce.
